Account Opt-Out Rules

If you would like to opt out of these rules, you need to be made aware that doing so will ban your IP address, your email, your entire account and will also remove every single little bit of information about you. This is to 100% ensure every last little bit about you that could get you doxxed is removed. The ban will last thirty (30) days, but if you don't associate yourself with Faraemon America other than on their community tabs, meaning you don't play the games and you don't purchase anything of ours directly on the Official Faraemon Store, then your information stays permanently deleted. This makes it inherently impossible to dox you directly from accessing random server files (which may or may not include our archives).


Account Basics in General

You must be 13 years or older (or the legal age in your country) to use our services. If your account gets terminated because of bad behavior (ergo you decide to try to implode the servers we have or you threaten other users), that's entirely on you. You may have up to 2 accounts on an IP Group address, and an IP Group address consists of up to 7 IP addresses that any of your devices can play on. Likewise, other people around you (e.g. neighbors or friends from school) can join your IP group which connects them to both your Mobile Client and your PC Clients.


What is a Mobile/PC Client?

A Mobile Client is any cellular device with an active GPS that is able to play our games. A PC Client is any non-cellular device that doesn't have a GPS but still has an internet connection, so they're able to play the game just not walking around (think of it like storage for your Faraemon).

Community Rules

Mainly, these are the rules we have set for people:

  1. No harassing, griefing, spamming, or doing anything that intentionally ruins the experience for others.
  2. If you're warned or banned, do not spam the server with pings or complaints—it'll only make things worse.
  3. Stay safe, respectful, and responsible. Everything you do comes back around to you.

We see activity logs. Being honest with us usually works out better than lying your way out of things.


Usage and Fair Play

You get at least 64 tokens automatically in a 24 hour period. you can have up to 128 at a time (excluding ones received from purchasing Token Packages). These tokens are used for communicating to the servers we have about information from GeoMapping to simply checking out your ban records. 2 tokens can be used to trade in for 1 Faracia (Packages to get Faracia are 50, 200, 500, 900, 1400 and 2000) and it costs about $0.50 USD, $0.70 CAD or $0.75 AUD for a package of 100 Faracia.

What you use your quota on is your business—but please don't spam weird backend commands. In-game commands are fine -- they're more than fine -- but outside of that alone, most commands we don't like and our server doesn't like either.


Prohibited Activities

You cannot try to break the system; if you want to get source code all you have to do is just ask. You yourself cannot try to reverse engineer our server or the backend. We will give you the open-source server code upon request. You also cannot sell your tokens to other users (albeit the only exception to this is from Bargaining, although that is a new feature coming soon), and you cannot sell your account for monetary value. I'm not sure why you would want to store 7 IP addresses in an IP group, then sell that information to someone else. Kinda defeats the purpose of anti-doxxing......


What can other people do?

Odds are that you have a few friends with computers that need some sort of, well, purpose. This implies they want to start up their own servers. Here's what you can do if you want to start your own server:

  1. You can paywall your server. Yup, that's legal. You can charge people to play. Just don't be greedy—10 Faracia is a decent start.
  2. You can ban, mod, and kick people however you like. No need to wait for 600 approval requests. Your server, your rules.
  3. You can tweak the economy. Token caps, Faracia exchange rates, etc. Want people to earn 256 tokens daily and buy 200 Faracia for 60 cents? Go for it. But remember—currencies don't transfer between servers.
  4. You can host merch. We sell shirts. You can sell whatever you want—as long as it's legal in your region. Backpack? Sure. $4M body pillow? Do it. Just... we'll silently judge you if we ever meet IRL.
